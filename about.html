Our story

Nik Naks was born from a late-night craving and a dream to put real, bold flavours into every bag. What started as a small kitchen experiment in 2020 has grown into a beloved snack brand known for our signature Simbas crunch — a playful, roaring twist on classic snacks. We combine locally sourced grains, spices, and a whole lot of heart to create treats that feel like home but taste like adventure.

From farmers' markets to being stocked in over 200 stores across Kenya, our journey is fuelled by snack lovers who believe that every bite should tell a story. Our name "Nik Naks" reflects the little moments — the ‘naks’ of joy between meals, the unexpected crunch that makes you smile.
